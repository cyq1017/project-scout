## Recommendation

Do not position a new skill as only an idea validator. Existing candidates
already cover that surface.

Prefer an evidence-backed opportunity discovery skill that combines:

- community pain signals
- prior-art and competitor mapping
- source coverage confidence
- China-relevant platforms such as Xiaohongshu and WeChat
- explicit build/adopt/borrow/fork/plugin recommendations

Likely decision: `Borrow` and `Extend`, not `Write New` from a blank slate.

## Transfer

Future opportunity-discovery work should start with a formal gate and include a
source profile covering skills registry, GitHub, web/product pages, community
sources, jobs, ecommerce reviews, and China-specific platforms.
